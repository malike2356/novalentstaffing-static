About Novalent Staffing

Your trusted partner for flexible staffing solutions across Hampshire

About Us

Our Mission

To connect Hampshire job seekers with businesses seeking flexible, part-time staffing solutions across Portsmouth, Southampton, Fareham and beyond — creating opportunities for candidates while helping businesses meet their staffing needs.

Our Vision

To become Hampshire's go-to staffing agency for flexible work solutions, known for understanding candidate needs and providing reliable, quality placements to local businesses.

Why We Started

Novalent Staffing Ltd was established to address the local demand for flexible, part-time staffing. We saw a gap: businesses needed reliable temporary and part-time staff, while many job seekers wanted work that fitted around other commitments. We bridge that gap with a quality-focused, personalised approach.
